LevitateRedoneinto Mod by kiskoller

Notice: Mod hasn't been thoroughly tested yet! Use it at your own risk!


How to install:

Copy LevitateRedoneinto your mods folder : DaggerfallUnity_Data\StreamingAssets\Mods

Enable the mod in Daggerfall Mod Manager.

How to use:

Make a new levitat spell in the spellmaker. Higher magnitudes will make you go faster.

How to uninstall:

Disable the mod, then remove the .dfmod file.
