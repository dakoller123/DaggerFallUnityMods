Clairvoyance Mod by kiskoller

Notice: Mod hasn't been thoroughly tested yet! Use it at your own risk!


How to install:

Copy Clairvoyance.dfmod into your mods folder : DaggerfallUnity_Data\StreamingAssets\Mods

Enable the mod in Daggerfall Mod Manager.

How to use:

Make a new spell with the spellmaker. It's called Detect Quest. It will show the quest target on your compass. Only works inside dungeons and for normal quest targets.
Some quests have no regular quest activators, for example Lysandus's Tomb.

How to uninstall:

Disable the mod, then remove the .dfmod file.
